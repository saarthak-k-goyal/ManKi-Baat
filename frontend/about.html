About ManKi-Baat

Our mission to help you understand your emotional weather

Why Track Your Moods?

ManKi-Baat is a gentle companion for your emotional journey. By recording your daily moods, you create a map of your inner landscape, helping you recognize patterns, celebrate joys, and navigate challenges with greater awareness.

Designed with care by a small team of developers who believe in the power of self-reflection, our app provides a simple, beautiful space to check in with yourself.
